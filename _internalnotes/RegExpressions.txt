Regex to convert a numbered list of links to headings into the headings:

        Replace this:
        ([0-9]\.) \[(.*?)\]\((.*?)\)\.

        with this:
        ### $2  {$3}\n


Regex to convert markdown URL to HTML URL:

        Search for:
           \[(.*?)\]\((.*?)\)
        replace with:
           <a href="$2">$1</a>
