
To move topic into the wiki:
    - build docstest
    - go to page in docstest
    - copy the body of the page onto the clipboard
    - create new wiki page
    - enter a title
    - paste the content
    - delete 'Learn about our products' if at top

To move topic from wiki into the docs:
    - in wiki menu, choose 'Storage Format' to reveal the html
    - copy the html to the clipboard
    - in docs page, replace from H1 to end of section with paste
    - if necessary, add back in the "on-prem" note at the top
    - globally replace https://docstest.splicemachine.com/ with empty-string to fix all urls
