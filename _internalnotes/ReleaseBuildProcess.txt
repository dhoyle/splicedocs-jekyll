
Step-by-step release build-and-upload process (using 2.7.0 as example)

1. Build 2.6.0 for docstest and generate sitemap

2. Upload build to 2.6.0/NewVersion

3. Build 2.6.1 for docstest and generate sitemap

4. Upload build to 2.6.1/NewVersion

5. Build 2.7.0 (new release) for docstest and generate sitemap

6. Combine sitemaps:
    cat sitemap.xxx.xml > _site/sitemap.xml
    edit extraneous lines out of _site/sitemap.xml

7. Upload build to 2.7.0/NewVersion
